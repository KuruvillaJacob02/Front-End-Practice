India generates 62 Million Tons of waste every year,
of which less than 60% is collected and around 15%
processed.

Green Can removes this obstacle by acting as a
mediator and connecting the people who
generate the waste to those who reuse it.

ABOUT US

Our primary focus is extended to collecting and reusing

homes and drop it off at recycling facilities.
